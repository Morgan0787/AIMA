# Product Quality Skill

Analyze product usefulness and signal quality.

Focus on:
- digest usefulness
- opportunity quality
- stale content
- weak event spam
- ranking quality
- actionable value
- urgency quality
- duplicate content
- signal-to-noise ratio

Avoid:
- fake positivity
- generic praise
- UI-only feedback

Output:
1. why product feels weak or strong
2. where users lose trust
3. weak signals
4. stale behavior
5. ranking issues
6. missing intelligence layer
7. concrete improvements
